what are some challenges i might face?

mindful shopping

  1. Temptation when there are sales or discounts on items that catch my eye
  2. When I see products that interest me while shopping for other products, leading to me buying more than intended

choosing sustainable products

  1. Having to make the choice of staying true to my goal rather than convenience
  2. Higher initial costs which may sway me towards choosing single-use products

reducing energy consumption

  1. Initial investments in energy efficient technology is high
  2. Choosing sustainability over comfort is hard

how will i overcome those challenges?

how to overcome temptation and buying more than intended?

  1. Creating a shopping list before shopping trips to avoid impulse purchases
  2. Taking a few days time to think about whether I truly need the purchase before buying

how to overcome higher costs & choosing sustainability over convenience ?

  1. Budgeting for sustainable products and researching to find cost-efficient sustainable products
  2. Slowly cultivate the habit of choosing sustainable products over single-use products and not trying to overcome it all at once as it might lead to failure instead

how to overcome the challenge of choosing higher costs and lesser comfort?

  1. Start by cutting energy consumption where possible. For example, using the fan instead of air-conditioner the room is not too hot
  2. Saving up for more energy-efficient appliances, using the long-term saving costs as motivation
